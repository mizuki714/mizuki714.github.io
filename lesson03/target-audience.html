Target Audience

The target audience for this site are people who live locally in the communities of Fish Haven, Preston, Soda Springs, and other surrounding communities who are interested in the local weather and events that may be happening in their community. Visitors will be from low income families ($15,000 anually) and students all the way up to midlde income retirees (incomes around $150,000 anually) looking for updates on their local weather.

Persona 1: Karen

Karen is a 35-year-old midlde class stay-at-home mother of 2. Often visiting relatives in Idaho. She is always using her tablet or cell phone to write reviews, research safe spaces for her children, ask important questions, speak to management, and ensure that the weather will be absolutely perfect for her meticulously scheduled family trips.

Persona 2: Steve

Steve is a 58-year-old single man who loves the outdoors and enjoys recreation such as fishing, boating, and other various outdoor activities in his community. He wants to keep himself updated on the activities in his local area, and keep up on all of the current events in his community.

Scenario 1

Steve needs to know if the weather in Fish Haven will be rainy or sunny so that he knows which kind of fishing gear to bring with him on his trip.

Scenario 2

Karen needs to know if the weather will be nice enough for a picnic on the weekend and if she needs to bring jackets.

Scenario 3

Steve is looking to see if there will be rain so he and his friends can go mudding.

Scenario 4

Karen is wondering what kind of clothing she needs to pack for her family on their trip to visit her in-laws in Preston.

Scenario 5

Karen is wondering what the weather in Soda Springs looks like so that she knows if she will need an umbrella or sunscreen.
