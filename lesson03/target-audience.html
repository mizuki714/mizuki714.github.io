Target Audience

Persona 1: Karen

Karen is a 35 year old stay-at-home mother of 2. Often going on vacation and visiting relatives in Idaho. She is always using her tablet or cell phone in order to write reviews, research safe spaces for her children, ask important questions, speak to management and also ensure that the weather will be absolutely perfect for her meticulously scheduled family trips.

Persona 2: Steve

Steve is a 58 year old man who loves the outdoors and enjoys recreation such as fishing, boating, and other various outdoor activities in his community. He wants to keep himself updated on the activities in his local area, as well as stayt updated on current events.
