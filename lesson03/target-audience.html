Target Audience

Persona 1: Karen

Karen is a 35-year-old midlde class stay-at-home mother of 2. Often going on vacation and visiting relatives in Idaho. She is always using her tablet or cell phone to write reviews, research safe spaces for her children, ask important questions, speak to management, and ensure that the weather will be absolutely perfect for her meticulously scheduled family trips.

Persona 2: Steve

Steve is a 58-year-old single man who loves the outdoors and enjoys recreation such as fishing, boating, and other various outdoor activities in his community. He wants to keep himself updated on the activities in his local area, and keep up on all of the current events in his community.

Scenario 1

Steve needs to know if the weather in Fish Haven will be rainy or sunny so that he knows which kind of fishing gear to bring with him on his trip.

Scenario 2

Karen needs to know if there will be any activites going on that will be safe and engaging for her children during their trip.

Scenario 3

Steve is looking for a nice place to take his lady friend for a date, that also will not break the bank.

Scenario 4

Karen is looking for accomodations for her family of four and wondering if there are any tourist locations that might support breastfeeding moms.

Scenario 5

Karen is wondering what the views in soda springs look like compared to the views in Fish Haven.
