Riding a Scooter on Cozumel Island in Mexico

POSTED ON MAY 8, 2018 BY JUSTCHASINGSUNSETS

Apart from knowing that scuba diving is awesome on Cozumel and it’s a major cruise docking point, I didn’t do much research on things to do in Cozumel, Mexico before I arrived. So as I walked from the ferry to the hostel, I was surprised at the number of agencies offering scooter and car rentals. I thought a car seemed a bit unnecessary. However, I noticed the beaches in town were all coral and rocky – not the white sand I’d imagined. The receptionist at the hostel confirmed I would need transportation if I intended to experience Cozumel’s sandy beaches. They offered scooter rentals, which is how I found myself riding a scooter on Cozumel the following day!
