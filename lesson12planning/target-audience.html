Target Audience

The target audience for this site are locals, walk-in tourists, enthusiasts, and cruise-based touring groups and partnerships who may like to do their homework before getting to Cozumel and to provide information about the services, requirements, and rental process.

Persona 1: Wade Wilson

Wade is a middle-aged cosplayer with an average middle class income of around $50,0000. Wade is about to get married and go on a honeymoon cruise with his soon to be wife Mercedes. He is really excited about this trip and wants it to be perfect. He heard about the availability of scooters and wants to check out the costs in order to plan exciting activities to do while also being able to get around quick and easy!

Persona 2: Paolo Valisari

Paolo is a 17 year old young man in high school with a lower annual income of around $30,000 Paolo thinks that a scooter will help him meet girls on the island. He is trying to convince his mother to allow him to rent a scooter while they are on the island.
