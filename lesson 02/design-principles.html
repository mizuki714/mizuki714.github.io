Design Principles

Contrast

PayPal

I think that the paypal website does a wonderful job of using contrast. You can see from the page, that they use blue and white to show contrast and also add a little yellow in order to make things pop. There is also plenty of whitespace and the buttons they want to you click are very visible. This makes navigation easy.

Visual Hierarchy

Barbie(Mattel)

Barbie's website uses images to draw you in, then you are able to see a large logo and advertisement for a product you can buy along with a large black button (that is contrasting) in order to focus you on to what they intedn you to do on this site, which is shop for their new products. YOu can also see important information is displayed as well as the navigation bar is large so that you can properly navigate to a specific product. This use of visual Hierarchy allows the user to look at the things that are most important rather than focus on what isnt.

Fitts' Law

GRUBHUB

Grubhub uses Fitts' Law in order to get right to the point. On their main page they have placed rather large boxes for you to enter your infromation in order to search for food nearby. They use the image of pizzas in the background to also draw you in to see the text boxes and buttons they need you to interact with.
